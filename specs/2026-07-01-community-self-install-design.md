# SEO OS community self-install package: design

Date: 2026-07-01
Status: Approved direction (self-install, maximum ease). Ready for plan review.
Owner: Nico

## Goal

Let an AI Ranking community member who already runs Hermes on a VPS (Hostinger or
similar) stand up their OWN copy of the SEO OS dashboard, connected to their own
Hermes, in about 15 minutes with two commands: one on their laptop, one on their
VPS. Everything else is automated or prompted.

Target user: technical enough to have Hermes running with per-client profiles, has
(or can create) a free Cloudflare account, can SSH to their VPS. Not expected to
edit config files by
